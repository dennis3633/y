Hostel Availability

Students who are joining SEKU University are required to pay a fee of Kenyan shillings 2400 to be booked in a school hostel. The university can only accommodate 90% of the students on a first-come, first-served basis upon clearance of tuition and accommodation fees.

Currently, SEKU has eight hostels available for booking:

  • Victoria Hostel
  • Turkwell Hostel
  • Elementaita Hostel
  • Athi Hostel
  • Baringo Hostel
  • Yatta Hostel
  • Magadi Hostel
  • Turkana Hostel

A number of the hostels have attached kitchens to serve the residents, while Kitchen One serves the rest of the hostels. Students are provided with well-prepared quality meals at affordable prices in the kitchens.

Meal Services

SEKU University provides well-prepared quality meals at affordable prices in the kitchens of the hostels. Students can enjoy a variety of meals during their stay on campus.
